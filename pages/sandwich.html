Italian Sub

This Italian sub sandwich is a perfectly serviceable sandwich for when you are too focused coding for too long to make a more quality meal. Don't let that put you off though this sandwich is made with only quality ingredients and is bound to make it into your regualar rotation.

Ingredients

  • 1 freshed baked white sub roll
  • 2 slices thin-sliced deli ham
  • 4 slices Deli style peperoni
  • 10 slices garlic and herb salami
  • 2 slices provolone cheese
  • Dijon mustard (to taste)
  • 1/2 cup shredded lettuce

Instructions

  1. Cut bread loaf in two
  2. spread dejon mustard evenly on bread in prefered amount
  3. put peperoni evenly spaced on bread
  4. place ham on bread
  5. place salami
  6. add cheese
  7. add lettuce evenly across sandwich
  8. Enjoy!
