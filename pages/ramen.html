Stove-Top Ramen

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • 1 pound chicken
  • 1 table spoon olive oil
  • 1 tea spoon salt
  • 1 tea spoon pepper
  • 1 table spoon garlic powder
  • 1 table spoon onion powder

prep

  • cut chicken into cubes
  • seperate ingredient portion to minimize hassle later

Instructions

  1. put olive oil in skillet and let heat till it begins to smoke
  2. add chicken and various seasoning to chicken and cook to it begins to brown
